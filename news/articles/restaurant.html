New Restaurant

There is a new restaurant in Zagreb. It is called Hashbrown.


Hashbrown is a new restaurant in Zagreb. It is located in the center of Zagreb, in the street of the same name.

They offer a wide variety of dishes, from traditional Croatian cuisine to international cuisine. They also offer a wide variety of drinks, from wine to beer. In addition to the food and drinks, they also offer a wide variety of desserts.
